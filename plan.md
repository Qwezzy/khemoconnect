# Project Brief: KhemoConnect 2.0 Website Redesign

## 1. Project Overview
KhemoConnect (a division of Khemo IT Solutions) is pivoting from a traditional web design/hosting agency into a **Modern Digital Transformation & Automation Partner** for South African SMEs. 

We no longer sell "commodity websites." We sell outcome-driven solutions: Website-as-a-Service (WaaS), AI-powered business automation, and secure Digital Workspaces (SharePoint/M365). The new website must reflect a premium, authoritative, and modern tech-forward brand.

## 2. Target Audience
* **Primary:** South African SME Owners, Founders, and Operations Managers (Medical practices, Legal firms, Logistics, Construction, Retail).
* **Pain Points:** Wasting time on manual admin tasks, old websites that don't generate leads, fear of POPIA compliance fines, remote-work communication breakdowns.
* **Goal:** They want to save time, increase revenue, and look like a top-tier brand without managing the tech themselves.
